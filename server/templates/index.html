Smart Caregiver IoT Application

Abstract & Selected Topic

HEALTHCARE - IoT related applications


Problem Statement

Patients that are bedridden are largely immobile and are increasingly dependent on others in their daily lives. Lack of control in their body movement and their environment can negatively impact the quality of life.


Expected Goal, outcome and scope of the project

Smart Caregiver IOT Application aims to improve the quality of life for immobile bedridden patients by providing basic environment and comfort needs without requiring physical movement.


  • Controlling of domestic appliances (Lights and Fans)
    • Automatic: Based on temperature (Fans) & timing of day (Lights)
    • Manual: Control appliances from application
  • Time based reminder to take medicine with light bulb and speaker

Raspberry Pi No. 1: Server. Communicates with Raspberry Pi No. 2 to receive environmental information, then sends commands to Raspberry Pi No. 3 to control appliances.

Raspberry Pi No. 2: Sensor. Detects temperature changes in the environment and keeps track of the sunset/sunrise time. Sends this information to Raspberry Pi No. 1.

Raspberry Pi No. 3: Fan and lights. Fan (speed/operation) and lights(operation) are adjustable based on the commands given by Raspberry Pi No. 1. Has a button to stop speaker/turn off light bulb once medicine is taken. (output)

Thermal Sensor: Determine the temperature of the room, adjust the fan power based on temperature benchmark.

LED Lights, Speaker: To flash and play voiceline as a reminder for patients to take medicine.
